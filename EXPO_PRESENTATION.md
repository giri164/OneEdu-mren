## ❓ Q&A Preparation (2-3 minutes)

**Common Questions & Answers**:

**Q: How does your AI differ from existing career platforms?**
"A: Most platforms use static questionnaires. Our AI analyzes current market trends, skill combinations, and provides dynamic recommendations that evolve with industry changes."

**Q: What's your biggest technical challenge?**
"A: Integrating multiple AI services while maintaining performance and ensuring data privacy. We solved this with service-oriented architecture and comprehensive error handling."

**Q: How scalable is your solution?**
"A: Built with cloud-native principles - MongoDB Atlas for database, Vercel/Render for hosting, with automatic scaling and global CDN."

**Q: What's your monetization strategy?**
"A: Freemium model - basic features free for students, premium features for professionals and institutions."

**Q: How do you ensure AI accuracy?**
"A: Continuous training with industry data, user feedback loops, and fallback mechanisms for reliability."
